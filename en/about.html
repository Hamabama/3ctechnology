Company History

The 3C Technology Company was founded in 2011 to promote the innovative «3C» dry mix technology for production of «3С» concrete. Our employees have over 20 years of experience in the production of cellular concrete. The total number of employees is 25 people, and all of them have higher professional education. Our specialists maintain contacts with the world’s leading producers of cellular concrete.

Our prime consultant is Tamara Ukhova, a candidate of technical sciences, an awardee of the State Prize, head of the cellular concrete laboratory at the Research Institute for Reinforced Concrete (Moscow).

Cellular concrete technology. Timeline:

2008 - creating and patenting of the gas-fibro concrete dry mix. The manufacturing factory: Dnepropetrovsk, Ukraine.

2010 - creating technology of dry-mix - «CFC» – «Cellular Fibro Concrete» production (Sofia, Bulgaria) and acquisition of a European patent.

2012 - creating technology of «Composite Cellular Concrete» dry mix production and patenting it in the Russian Federation and in Europe.

2015 - acquisition of a «3C» dry-mix production technology patent in China;

2015 - certification for compliance with the applicable national standards (GOST), for health and fire safety;

2015 - opening of the «3C» dry-mix production factory in the Kaliningrad Region; the daily output - 70 tons;

Each subsequent technology is an upgraded version of the previous solution.

Technical Support

Our specialists will assist you in designing a house, cottage, residential complex, road, landscape, or anything else where you can use «3С» concrete. They will also help you to calculate costs.

Experts’ assistance

Our experts will select raw materials required for «3С» dry mix so that your project would be economically beneficial and would meet your specifications.

They will conduct detailed studies in our laboratory, select the optimum composition formula and special additives.

They will select and recommend the material composition for the specific project. You can rely on their support during the construction period.

On-site services

Our specialists can visit your construction site to give advice on placing of workers, optimization of technological processes, using of equipment, implementing of quality control measures for successful completion of the project. They will show you how to use the equipment and will find optimum engineering solutions.
